The Norman's won the battle

The outcome for the Norman

The Norman build castles, suppressed rebellions, and replaced many nobles. This cemented William's power in England giving him the throne for years to come.

The outcome for the English/Saxons

Most English nobles either died in the battle or fled to other neighboring countries. They were stripped of their land which was given to Norman nobles.
